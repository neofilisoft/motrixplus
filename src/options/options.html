About Motrix+

Motrix Plus — smart download manager extension.

M+
Motrix+
Version 1.0.0

Motrix+ is a browser extension that brings the power of aria2's multi-connection downloading to your browser — with smart bandwidth monitoring, automatic speed optimization, and latency-aware tuning.

Forked from agalwood/Motrix (MIT License). Rebranded and extended as Motrix Plus.
